Admissions Process

Our admissions process typically includes the following steps:

  1. Submit an Application Form online or in person.
  2. Complete an Entrance Examination .
  3. Interview with School Officials .
  4. Receive an Admission Decision.
  5. Complete the Enrollment Process.
